Discover the project
  1. 01.

    Iceberg is a transmedia documentary that uncovers humanity’s hidden capacity to tackle the climate crisis.

  2. 02.

    Through film, podcasts, shared initiatives, and crowdfunding, Iceberg challenges the narrative of helplessness and reveals the global solutions already emerging beneath the surface.

  3. 03.

    It's an invitation to reimagine the future and reconnect with our ability to transform it.

Around the world, thousands of initiatives are quietly revolutionizing our predatory system, though— like an iceberg—they often remain hidden beneath the surface.

Projects across the globe that challenge the status quo and reimagine what’s possible. From regenerative economy to sustainable technology and community-driven solutions, these initiatives reveal the power of collective action in building a resilient, sustainable future.

  • POSITIVE MONEY

    Redesign the global economic system for social justice and a livable planet

    Economics London, United Kingdom 2010
  • TIME USE INITIATIVE

    The global initiative promoting time policies and the right to time to reduce time inequality and enhance health and overall wellbeing.

    Human Wellfare Barcelona, Spain 2014
  • ESCUELA DE CATEURA

    School and orchestra which uses instruments made from waste to combine recycling and social inclusion.

    Recycling, education, social inclusion Asunción, Paraguay 2006
